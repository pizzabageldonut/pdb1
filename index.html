Welcome to the Pizza Bagel Donut Game!

How to Play

Guess unique numbers in 8 tries. Easy uses 3 digits, Medium uses 4, and Hard uses 5. After each guess, you will receive clues:

🍕 PIZZA: A number is correct and in the right spot.
🥯 BAGEL: A number is correct, but in the wrong spot.
🍩 DONUT: A number is not in the puzzle at all.

Example

Answer: 507

GUESS
0 6 7
🍕 🥯 🍩

7 is correct and in the right spot → 🍕
0 is correct but in the wrong spot → 🥯
6 is not in the answer → 🍩
