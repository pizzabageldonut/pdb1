Welcome to the Pizza Bagel Donut Game!

How to Play

The goal is to guess a hidden 3-number code to open the Pizza Box in 6 tries. After each guess, you will receive three clues:

🍕 PIZZA: One number is correct and in the right spot.
🥯 BAGEL: One number is correct, but in the wrong spot.
🍩 DONUT: One number is wrong (it is not in the code at all).

⚠️ The Shuffled Clue Rule

The clues (Pizza, Bagel, Donut) DO NOT match the order of your numbers.

Example: If you guess 1-2-3 and the clue is "Pizza," it doesn't mean the number 1 is the Pizza. It just means one of those three numbers is in the right spot!

All numbers in the code are unique.
