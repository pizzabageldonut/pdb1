Welcome to the Pizza Bagel Donut Game!

How to Play

Guess the numbers in 6 tries. Easy uses 3 digits, Medium uses 4, and Hard uses 5. After each guess, you will receive clues:

🍕 PIZZA: A number is correct and in the right spot.
🥯 BAGEL: A number is correct, but in the wrong spot.
🍩 DONUT: A number is not in the puzzle at all.

⚠️ The Shuffled Clue Rule

The clues do not match the order of your numbers. They only tell you how many of each clue you earned.

All numbers in the code are unique.
